Services

Free Changing Rooms

Changing rooms and bathroom facilities are available to any temple patrons free of charge.

Playroom and Babysitting

Free playroom access for all temple patrons with babysitting available for a low cost, with an additional discount for hotel patrons.

Themed Reception

Every reception area is themed according to the local temple's history. A great place to learn about the city and growth of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the area.

Family History Center

Full featured family history center, with several computers and printers to assist temple patrons find relatives and print ordinance cards. Also doubles as a buisness center for hotel patrons.
