Reception

Floor Plan Options

  • Outdoor Tent

    Set directly outside the hotel. Perfect for those who love the outdoors! Only avalable in fair weather.

  • Indoor, Bride and Groom Apart

    Positions the bride and groom seperate from the other tables and guests right in front of the dance floor. Ideal for large gatherings.

  • Indoor, Bride and Groom Interspersed

    Places the bride and groom at a table with guests, with tables all around the dance floor. Perfect for smaller occasions

Catering Options

  • Surf and Turf

    Steak and seafood, served with choices of baked potatoes and toppings, pasta, salad, and your choice of desserts.

  • Italian Style

    Lasangna, fettuccine alfredo, and spaghetti with choices of garlic bread, breadsticks, salad, and your choice of desserts.

  • Authentic Hispanic

    Street tacos, enchiladas, and tostadas with choices of beans, rice, salad, and your choice of desserts.

  • Self Provided

    We open our facilities to you so that you can provide your own custom meal plan for your special day!

Decoration Options

  • Enchanted Forest

    We transform the room into an enchanted forest, complete with live shrubs on the outskirts of the room and fresh flowers at every table.

  • Beautiful Sunset

    Shades of red, yellow, and orange cover the room, imitating a clear sunset on a tranquil beach, with warm lighting and beach themed tables.

  • Driven Snow

    Pure white decorations for everything. White roses on every table with rustic wood tables to help accentuate the white and give it personality.
