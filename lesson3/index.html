Site Name

Weather1Preston

Site Purpose

This site's purpose is to provide accurate and timely information about the weather and activities dependent on the weather to residents or tourists interested in visiting the area. When visiting this site, users should be able to find daily and weekly forcasts for Preston and surrounding communities, monitor the status of approching storms, and see relevent photos of the area.
