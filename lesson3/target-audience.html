Audience Description

The audience for this website is very broad, loosely encompassing anyone who has activies planned outdoors. This ranges from youth groups seeking to do outdoor activities or plan camping trips in the next couple days, to farmers relying on the weather to plan their weekly schedules, to prospecive residents looking to see long term weather patterns, and more.

Personas

John

John is an average blue collar worker who enjoys spending his free time outside. He often checks the weather to plan his weekend activites. When he does so, he wants to see temperature, wind speed, and precipitation.

Sarah

Sarah is a single mother with two children. She is currently looking to move, and a crucial factor for her is climate. She wants to see long term trends in weather, as well as past notable storm/weather events.

Scenarios

Tourists

A tourist is checking the weather to see if it will be worth stopping in this area for a picnic and family outing in a week. Wants to check temperature and chance of precipitation.

Farmers

One local farmer needs to harvest his crop within the next two weeks, but also wants to let it grow as much as possible. He needs to see chance of precipitation and if there are any storms presicted to come through

Residents

A local resident jogs with a friend every week. He checks the weather to check odds of precipitation and whether it will be cold enough for ice to form. Also checks humidity.

Boy Scouts

Check weather regularly to plan outdoor activities and camping trips. Need to be able to check weather in a fairly wide area. Mostly concerned about storms, however temperature and precipitation is useful as well.

Prospective Residents

Looking for a new place of residence. They're less concerned about the weather in a day or even in a week, but wants to be able to see past weather to see if the climate will fit within their criteria.
